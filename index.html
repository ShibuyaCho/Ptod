✦ How It Works

From white shirt
to work of art

Every piece goes through a hands-on, labor-of-love process. Here's how your custom creation comes to life.

1
You Reach Out
Message me on Facebook or call/text with your idea. Tell me colors, size, style.
2
We Design
I figure out the best pattern and color layout to bring your vision to life.
3
Hand-Dyed
Every piece is folded, tied, and dyed by hand. This is where the magic happens.
4
It's Yours
Reveal, rinse, dry — and your one-of-a-kind piece is ready to pick up or ship!
